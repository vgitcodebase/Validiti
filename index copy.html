F.A.Q

Frequently Asked Questions

  • How do I know if my idea is worth anything?

    Whether you have a problem or a fully operational solution, we will gladly take a look and if we find it to be marketable, that's awesome. If not, maybe we made a new friend.

  • What happens if my idea can be marketed and made profitable?

    We start with a patentability test to see what protections we can secure. Next, we package the solution to market it to customers or for financing, if needed.

  • How much of my solution will I own when it comes to market?

    We'll split the equity (50/50) that remains after any necessary financing is secured.

  • What if I only have a problem and no idea where to start on a solution?

    That's good enough for us! Let us know what your problem is and if we can solve it, then maybe we'll have a business! If the solution has no marketability, then you don't owe us anything beyond a recommendation.

  • Will this cost me anything?

    No.

  • Okay so how do I get started?

    Just fill out the short contact form and we will reach out!
