What is Trinity and how can it benefit your organization?


Trinity is a simple product to adopt.




Trinity exists as a secondary read and write system that processes data more efficiently, without interfering with your current workflow.


Trinity is a lightweight, hardware-enabled data management system.




It utilizes legacy hardware, with minimal footprint. Two tiers of service that losslessly reduce contextual files by 15 or 30% (Or offer 24% or 39% in usable storage, for space occupied by contextual data). Trinity files are always indexable and computational, and offer a unified, non-categorical search feature.

Trinity increases the safety of your data.




Trinity manages data based upon its unique characteristics, rendering it non-normalized. Therefore, generalized hacks aren't applicable and Trinity data must be targeted specifically.
